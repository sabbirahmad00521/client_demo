Bibiana is passionate about merging user insights and understanding of human behaviour to build meticulously deliverable products. She works with a variety of clients in the financial, pharmaceutical, B2B, non-profit, and sports/entertainment industries. By designing systems that cater to human needs, she understands that empathy is a key driving force to launching products addressed from users' perspectives. Using human-centered design in practice, she knows that every decision is impactful.
