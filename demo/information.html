Hi, I'm Javon - A New Orleans based UX Designer and Researcher with a background in Branding. I have a Business Degree, but have been drawn to the world of design since I was a child. I've been designing graphics, websites for many years and have been a self-taught graffiti artist since I was 9 years old. Being heavily inspired by so many different cultures due to the fact of growing up in Los Angeles has allowed me to work with big companies such as Facebook and Red Hat, as well as many small businesses and start ups.

      With UX Design not being an option for a degree during my time of undergrad, I decided to take on the opportunity of an immersive UX program at Springboard, and just over 600+ hours of training here I am. I want to thank you for viewing my portfolio and YES! I am for hire.
