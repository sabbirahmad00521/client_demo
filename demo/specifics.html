Specifics
Of Our Vessels

Simple concept for A better efficiency

  • Commonly used marine building materials (Aluminum)
  • True 'Plug & Play' capabilities (less than 24 hours required to replace main engine without dry-docking)
  • Designed to use off-the-shelf equipment

Improved
Reliability

  • Machinery redundancy
  • Fuel purifying system with triple filtering
  • Increased cooling capacity
  • Anti-sea growth system
  • Wide range of operating conditions (i.e., climate, wave height, etc.)

Operational
Friendliness

  • Large deck to accept various types of baskets for personnel transfers and cargo
  • Bow designed for boat landing access (wide and flat - no stairs)
  • Waterjet system with station-keeping mode to increase stability during cargo transfers
  • User-friendly controls
  • Optional DP2 (dynamic positioning)

Comfort

  • Active air cushion motion damping
  • Resiliently mounted cabins
  • Increased A/C capacity
  • Large Business Class seats
  • Entertainment systems (i.e. large screen TVs, Internet through WiFi, video on demand system, etc.)

Environment

  • Lower carbon footprint due to lower fuel consumption
  • Engines are IMO Tier II compliant with possibility to upgrade to IMO Tier III / U.S. EPA Tier 4
