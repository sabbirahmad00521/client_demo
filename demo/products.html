CALLING PLANS
Call Anywhere in the World

We offer flexible choices for International Long Distance (ILD). Whether you call one destination regularly or call all over the world, we have you covered. Our International Flexibility Plan allows subscribers to call anywhere in the world paying only for minutes you use. Our Unlimited Global Plan is perfect for subscribers who call up to 15 numbers in specific destinations most often.

PayGo Plan

To any destination worldwide. More minutes to the places you call most with crystal-clear quality. The PayGo Plan can be used with any voice plan.

  • Less than 2 cents per minute to select destinations in Mexico
  • Add funds when you need or sign up for auto-recharge to always have funds available
  • No expiration date
Unlimited Global Plan

No contracts, fees, cards or pins. You'll have unlimited access to 55+ destinations* for just $5.

  • Call up to 15 numbers per 30 day period
  • The 15 numbers are the first 15 numbers completed to each month
  • The 15 number list resets at each new billing cycle
  • If an 16th number is dialed, then it will work just like PayGo. If the PayGo is not funded, you will be directed to add funds.
  • The Unlimited Plan can be purchased at any time in your monthly service plan and lasts for 30 days.
