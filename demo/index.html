What is a Public Adjuster?

  • A Public Adjuster works for you, not the insurance company. Insurance companies have the benefit of representatives protecting their interests – so should you!

  • We are not employed by, and we do not represent the interests of any insurance company.

  • We represent the best interests of you, the policy holder, exclusively.

Water Damage

Every year hundreds of thousands of water damage claims are made in the country, making it one of the most common type of property insurance claims.

Vehicle Impact

Cars and trucks crashing into homes and businesses occur more often than you’d expect, causing extensive damages.

Fire Damage

The recovery process after a fire hits your home or business is certainly a difficult one. Even a small fire that is quickly brought under control can create a claim.

Hurricane Damage

Over the past few years insurers have seen an increase in the amount and severity of hurricane damage reports.

Mold Damage

Most home insurance policies cover mold damage if it was caused by a covered peril, which could be a sudden and unexpected burst pipe, a storm, or the result of fire control measures.

Vandalism/Theft

Vandalism may be a misdemeanor or felony offense depending on the amount of the property damage and state law. The police report may also be requested by your insurance company.
