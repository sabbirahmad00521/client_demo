When disaster strikes, which is quite often, losses occur. This can include damage from hurricanes, heavy rain storms, burst pipes from freezing weather and many other occurrences.

Although you may want to repair your damage right away, insurance companies require a process which is not easy. The complexity and amount of forms required can be difficult and homeowners tend to take whatever money is initially offered.

We have the experience and knowledge to interpret policy language to assure that you receive a proper and full settlement for all covered damages.

Vehicle Impact

Cars and trucks crashing into homes and businesses occur more often than you’d expect, causing extensive damages.

Freezing Damage

Burst pipes from freezing weather or snow and ice build up can cause substantial damage to homes or businesses.

Storm Damage

When disaster strikes, which is quite often, losses occur. This can include damage from hurricanes, heavy rain storms, and other natural occurrences.

Water Damage

Every year hundreds of thousands of water damage claims are made in the country, making it one of the most common type of property insurance claims.

Mold Damage

Most home insurance policies cover mold damage if it was caused by a covered peril, which could be a sudden and unexpected burst pipe, storm, or the result of fire control measures.

Fire Damage

The recovery process after a fire hits your home or business is certainly a difficult one. Even a small fire that is quickly brought under control can create a claim.

Hurricane Damage

Over the past few years we have seen an increase in the amount and severity of hurricanes.
